In the realm of photography & image making, my interests span across various genres including; portraiture, landscape, and studio work. I am also eager to learn more about the use of alternative materials and processes. I am drawn to the use of both digital photography & analog film, with a special interest in black and white imagery.

My goal is to create an atmosphere that explores the many aspects of the human experience, while also illuminating the connections that exist between individuals and the world around us. Through the use of visual language that conveys the emotions, experiences, personalities, and overall themes of the subjects within my photographs, I aim to highlight the beauty in the raw and unpolished moments of life.

My work seeks to evoke a sense of shared experience in the viewer, while also capturing the beauty and complexity of the world we inhabit.
